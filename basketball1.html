STORY

BEYOND

HOOPS AFRICA

Beyond Hoops Africa is a sport for development organization using basketball and life-skills to empower youth and children. BHA rides on sport’s ability to educate, empower and inspire youth from all walks of life. Sport is inherently about drawing on, developing and showcasing young people’s strengths and abilities. We aim to shine a light on what youth can do, rather than what they cannot do. This strength-based approach promotes hope and encourages youth and their communities to adopt a positive outlook. Our current programs are run in Kenya.

OUR VISION

To use basketball and purposeful play as a platform to ensure that children and youth have access to structured sporting activities and create safe space environment where they learn meaningful skills that they can use on and off the field.
02

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to use the power of sport to educate, inspire and empower youth and their communities to reach their full potential. Empowering youth and children is an obligation not an option.
03

Our Core Values

DIVERSITY We know that by embracing differences, actively including a variety of voices, and uniting together we can solve our community’s most complex problems.

RESPECT

 We believe in the value of treating our staff, partners, supporters and participants respectfully. This means assuming positive intent, practicing direct and supportive communication, maintaining confidentiality and honesty, and working toward mutual understanding.

INTEGRITY

 We are accountable to the people and partners we humbly serve, transparently sharing our results, stories and lessons

SPORTSMANSHIP

 We teach our participants about winning or losing with grace. Our programs teach participants that they must know and abide by rules of any sport they play and be able to conduct themselves in a kind and respectful manner towards others
